About Our Laboratories

Comprehensive Infrastructure

GMU CSE maintains over 180 computing systems, from high-performance machines to legacy setups, supporting courses from fundamentals to AI, Data Science, Cloud, and Cybersecurity.

Recent Additions

New I-Active AIOs (12th Gen i5, 16GB RAM), GPU-enabled workstations, and networking hardware enhance hands-on learning and research.

Software & Tools

Labs include dual-boot setups, Anaconda, PyTorch, TensorFlow, Wireshark, Packet Tracer, and cloud/containerized environments for experiments.

Research & Collaboration

Facilities support faculty-student research, capstone projects, and industry collaborations with structured access and mentorship.
